About This Recipe

Our Double Chocolate Brownies are the ultimate indulgence for chocolate lovers. These rich, fudgy brownies feature both cocoa powder and chunks of premium chocolate for an intense chocolate experience in every bite.

With a perfectly crackly top and a dense, moist center, these brownies strike the ideal balance between cake-like and fudgy textures that will satisfy even the most discerning chocolate connoisseurs.

What to Expect

  • Intensely rich chocolate flavor from premium cocoa and chocolate chunks
  • Perfect balance between fudgy and cake-like textures
  • Signature crackly top that chocolate lovers crave
  • Decadent dessert that's surprisingly simple to make
  • Versatile recipe that can be customized with nuts or other mix-ins
