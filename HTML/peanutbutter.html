About This Recipe

Our Peanut Butter Cookies are the perfect blend of sweet and salty with a rich, nutty flavor that peanut butter lovers will adore. These cookies have a wonderfully soft and chewy texture with a slightly crisp exterior.

Whether you enjoy them with a glass of milk or as an afternoon treat, these classic cookies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and bring back nostalgic memories.

What to Expect

  • Rich peanut butter flavor in every bite
  • Perfect balance of sweet and salty
  • Soft and chewy texture with classic criss-cross pattern
  • Simple ingredients you likely already have in your pantry
  • Easy to make - great for baking with kids!
