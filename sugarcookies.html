    Best Sugar Cookies

    • All-Purpose Flour – measured and then sifted into a bowl
    • Baking Powder – the main raising ingredient
    • Fine Sea Salt – or table salt
    • Granulated Sugar – for both the cookie dough and extra for
      rolling the dough balls in.
    • Large Eggs
    • Pure Vanilla Extract – for depth of flavor

    Step one:

    1. Preheat oven to 375° F. (190.56°C).

    Step two:

    1. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar
    2. and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well
    3. after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in morsels and nuts. Drop by rounded
    4. tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
